Empty / Tokenized Emptiness

Before the chain.

Before the chain, the world strained under the weight of borrowed conviction and over-inflated trust.

Fiat burned with the glow of printed certainty. Debt swelled. Markets rose on fumes.

A ledger of obligation stacked upon the whispers of baseless faith.

And when belief in faith and trust finally faltered, something else surfaced from the cracks.
