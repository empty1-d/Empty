Genesis of the Blockchain

A brief history of faith, leverage and emptiness

In the beginning, Satoshi created the Bitcoin — and saw that it was good. Blocks formed, and faith was forged in code. Faith in a new era of digitized sovereignty, of tokenized liberation. From that faith, a generation of new believers emerged — they who toiled in the shadows, turning belief into conviction.
the first believers
The Miners
They stacked hashes in the dark, chasing block rewards at the edge of thermodynamic sanity. Racks hummed, fans screamed, and electricity became tithe. In return, they received more than coins — they received proof.
when capital noticed
The Custodians of Order
It was only a matter of time before others arrived — those who bargain with governance in pursuit of asymmetry. They declared themselves the quiet stewards of value, the ones who would bring order to the chaos of keys and chains. Funds were raised. Vaults were built. Prospectuses were drafted. Tokenized wealth would rise without end, they said. But where wallets swell, greed is never far behind.
the charts became scripture
The Traders’ Belief
From the hunger for more, a new form of faith emerged. They worshipped the candle, feared the wick. Charts and leverage became their holy texts, patterns the crucible of their prophecies. Some prayed to support. Others knelt to resistance.
beyond risk, beyond reason
The DEGENS
And then, from within the traders’ ranks, a final sect arose: Those who feared nothing and restrained nothing. Volatility was their codec, chaos their altar. Memes became their icons, tokens their temples. Where markets burned, they laughed — for fire was proof of faith.
“HODL.” “Buy the dip.” “Diamond hands.” “To the moon.”
Words once invoked for perseverance, conviction, courage… As cycles turned and the illusion of scarcity thinned, they eroded into slogans of survival in portfolios that only knew one direction.
From the ashes of euphoria, a quiet truth emerges: For all the noise, for all the faith, for all the unshaken grip… the wallets remain still — numbers that shimmer without weight. And the diamond hands — cold, perfect, unyielding — remain  $EMPTY.
disclaimer / not financial advice

Fair warning: you may feel an urge to HODL, buy the dip, or even diamond-hand $EMPTYCOIN.
